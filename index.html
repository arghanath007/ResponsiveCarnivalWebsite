Program Schedules

Sur Sargam

Date: Dec 10th
Time allotted for each performance is 3 – 4 minutes. Participants will not be allowed to refer to the lyrics while singing. The participants should arrange their own musical instruments if required in the performance.Choice of song is open to the participants but the song should not have any slangs or derogatory language.

String Symphony

Date: Dec 10th
This is a solo competition and not open to ensembles (duets, trios, quartets).Time allotted for each performance is 3–5 minutes.No accompaniment or pre-recorded music shall be allowed. No Keyboard. Instruments of western origin adopted to the India Raga system are allowed.

Malang-e-Mridang

Date: Dec 10th
This is a solo competition.The classical dance can be from any of the approved schools of dance, such as Kathak,Kathakali, Bharat Natyam,Manipuri, Kuchipudi, Mohinittam, Odissi.Time allotted for each performance is 4 – 5 minutes.The participants will be judged on- Taal and Rhythm,Choreography, Technique,Abhinaya or Expression,Hand & eye movements,Footwork,Costumes and Overall Impression.

Feet Beat

Date: Dec 10th
This is a group performance competition with maximum of 7 Participants.Time allotted for each performance is 4–5 mins.Western free style or group Dance–any styles.Bollywood songs are not allowed.There should be no vulgarity and provocation in lyrics.The participants will be judged on-Theme,Presentation,Versatility,Stage Utilization,Costume.

Rangmanch Zindegika

Date: Dec 10th
This is a group performance competition with a maximum of 7 Participants.Time allotted for each performance is 5 – 7minutes.The main language may be either Bengali, Hindi or English or a combination.The script can be original or an adaptation.External music is allowed.No props will be provided, however teams may use their own props.Probition of any vulgarity with the performance or words, otherwise marks would be deduction or disqualified.

Fashion Show

Date: Dec 10th
This is a group performance competition with a maximum of 8 Participants.Time allotted for each performance is 7 minutes.The fashion show will be based on any Indian Theme.Each group should have one tag line representing their group and the theme.Negative marking if participants exceed time limit Music track for the presentation has to be arranged by the group.Vulgarity is strongly prohibited.

Group Band

Date: Dec 10th
This is a group performance competition with a maximum of 8 Participants Time allotted for each performance is 5 minutes.The event is open for all music genres.Original compositions are allowed. Print out of lyrics to be provided in advance.No pre-recorded sequence can be played on stage.Use of vulgar language or any obscenity will lead to on-the-spot disqualification of the band.

Sutradhar/Anchor

Date: Dec 10th
This is a solo competition.Time allotted for each performance is 3 minutes.Medium of expression will be Bengali, Hindi or English.The Topic shall be given on the spot.Reading from paper/other resources such as mobile phone/ tabs etc. is not permissible any shall lead to immediate disqualification.The participants will be judged on- Introduction,Knowledge,Presentation,Pronunciation,Body Language, Public Interaction, Confidence.

Recitation

Date: Dec 10th
This is a solo competition.Time allotted for each performance is 3 minutes.Participant must Present her/himself well and be attentive. Use good posture. Be confident and make a direct,connection with the audience Poem presented must not touch on sensitive issues such as races, religion, stereotyping, politics, etc.Participant will be allowed to read from the text of the poem, but bonus marks will be given to those that can memorize the poem.Props, Backdrops and Sound effects are not allowed.

WINGS OF IMAGINATION

Date: Dec 10th
This is a solo competition.Time allotted for each performance is 5 – 7 minutes.Subject for the sketch shall be given on-the-spot.Size of the sketch will be half imperial size drawing paper (22 inches X 15 inches).The sketching will have to be captured on time lapse video.Presentation will be online.The participants will be judged on- Thematic Relevance,Originality,Design and Creative Elements,Visual Execution,Physical Composition – skill of line work/brushstroke.

IN-QUIZ-ITIVE

Date: Dec 10th
This is a solo competition.Quiz will be conducted by a Quiz Master of repute.Judges will be selected by the institutes (intra-level).All Institute teams will go through Rapid Fire / Written questions in preliminary round.Six teams will go through to next round / finals.Finals will be oral with audio- visual questions.The Specific rules regarding evaluation procedure, time to reply a particular answer and the type of round will be given before the actual start of the competition.

WAR OF THE MINDS

Date: Dec 10th
There will be lottery on basis of which participants will speak.Each Institute will be represented by the two debaters, one will speak FOR, while another will speak AGAINST the motion.Medium of expression can be Bengali, Hindi or English.Topic of Debate will be announced 24 hours in advance.Paper reading is not allowed.For the benefit of the students, the respected judges will debate the same topic as an exhibition round immediately after the event.

PHOTOGRAPHY COMPETITION

Date: Dec 10th
All colleges are requested to exhibit 10 classy photographs in A4 size at the venue of Gala Event for which a corner each will be allocated Topics are:-
a. Pandemic 2020
b. Campus Life
c. Colours of Nature
The participants will need to bring his / her own digital camera of not more than 12 mega pixels.The digital camera should have a memory card which will be formatted by the judges before the commencement of the contest.The time limit is two and a half hours.The participant has to capture 5 photographs on the theme announced on the spot by the judges.No mixing, matching or morphing of photographs is permitted.Software such as Photoshop etc. for enhancing images is not permitted.Digital images will be evaluated on the basis of- Impact,CompositionTechnical, Quality,Suitability for the specific theme.

WEBSITE DESIGNING FOR SPIRIT-JIS

Date: Dec 10th
There will be two rounds for this event Round-1 will consist of 30 questions on HTML and CSS. All questions will be in the format of multiple choice questions. Duration for this will be 30 Minutes Max.In Round-2, shortlisted participants from Round 1 will need to design website based on any one of competition topics in 90 minutes using permitted development tools Competition Topics:
a.Incredible India
b.Digital India
c.e-Resources in the field of education
Permitted Tools:
a.Application software (FrontPage, Publisher, Dreamweaver, Photoshop etc.)
b.Programming Language (Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), Java Script, etc.
Maximum 2 members per group.Participants need to design a 5+ pages website based on the competition topic. Students have to design a web-site on any one of the specified topics and present it to the judges.
